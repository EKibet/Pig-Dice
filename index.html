Pig Dice


GAME RULES:

    ✓The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

    ✓In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he/she whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT score

    ✓BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his/her CURRENT score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

    ✓The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his/her ROUND score. After that, it's the next player's turn

    ✓The first player to reach 100 points on ROUND score wins the game

    ✓To start the game,fill in the fields then click on PLAY button
